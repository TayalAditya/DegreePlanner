## Important Clarifications

### ISTP is NOT MTP
- MTP: Required, technical project, 8 credits (3+5)
- ISTP: Optional, socio-technical project, 4 credits

### ISTP Replaces Course Credits, Not MTP
- Choosing ISTP does NOT reduce MTP requirement
- You still do BOTH MTP-1 (3) and MTP-2 (5)
- ISTP is ADDITIONAL 4 credits (or you get +4 FE if skipped)

### Semester 8 Can Be Heavy
- If you choose ISTP in Sem 8: MTP-2 (5) + ISTP (4) + other courses = 9+ credits
- If you skip ISTP: MTP-2 (5) + other courses = 7-8 credits
- Plan accordingly!

## Eligibility for ISTP

### For Semester 6 ISTP
- No prerequisites
- Can take anytime in semester 6
- Does NOT require 90 credits yet

### For Semester 8 ISTP
- Must have completed MTP-1 (semester 7)
- Must have 90+ credits completed
- Must be in final year (semester 8)

### For MTP
- Must have 90+ credits completed (by end of sem 6)
- Must be in Semester 7 or later
- Mandatory for all BTech

## FAQ

**Q: Can I do both ISTP and MTP simultaneously?**
A: Yes! In Semester 8, you can take both MTP-2 (5) + ISTP (4) together.

**Q: If I skip ISTP, can I take +4 FE credits in any semester?**
A: Yes! The 4 credits can be distributed as Free Electives in any semester.

**Q: Can I change my mind about ISTP?**
A: If you chose Sem 6, you can't change later. If you chose Sem 8 or Skip, you can decide in Sem 8.

**Q: Does ISTP affect MTP?**
A: No. ISTP is completely independent. You still do full MTP (3+5) credits.

**Q: Is ISTP graded differently?**
A: Same grading as any course. It's just 4 credits like other electives.

**Q: Can I do ISTP in Semester 5?**
A: No. ISTP can only be in Semester 6 or Semester 8.
